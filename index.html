About Sarvayogam School

Four decades of nurturing talent, building character and transforming lives in Ahmedabad.

Our Story

Shaping Lives Since 1985

Sarvayogam School was founded in 1985 with a simple yet powerful mission — to make quality English-medium education accessible to every child in Ahmedabad. Located in the heart of Gulbai Tekra, we have grown from a small institution to one of the area's most respected schools.

With a rating of 4.6 on Google and 93+ verified reviews, our reputation is built on trust, consistency and a genuine love for teaching. Our motto "Discover Talent" reflects our commitment to finding and nurturing the unique potential in every student.

  • Academic excellence with holistic personality development
  • Safe, inclusive and nurturing campus environment
  • Affordable quality education for all socio-economic backgrounds
  • Strong community ties with parents and alumni
  • Regular extracurricular activities, sports and cultural programs

How To Apply

Simple 4-Step Process

Visit or Call Us

Come to our campus in Gulbai Tekra or call us at 094287 37790 to speak with our admissions team.

Fill Application Form

Complete the admission form available at our office or download it from our contact page. Fill all details carefully.

Document Submission

Submit required documents: birth certificate, previous school report card, transfer certificate and passport photos.

Confirm Admission

Pay the admission fee and collect your child's ID and schedule. Welcome to the Sarvayogam family!

Documents Required

What To Bring

📄

Birth Certificate

Original birth certificate of the child along with one photocopy.

📊

Previous Report Card

Last academic year's mark sheet or progress report from the previous school.

📋

Transfer Certificate

TC from previous school if transferring mid-session or from another institution.

🪪

Aadhar Card

Child's Aadhar card copy for identity verification as per government norms.

📸

Passport Photos

4 recent passport-size photographs of the child (white or light-colored background).

🏠

Address Proof

Electricity bill, ration card or any government-issued residential address proof.
